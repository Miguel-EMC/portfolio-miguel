## About This Project

Mobile app that goes beyond traditional HR processes by analyzing meaningful data about candidates and teams, empowering better hiring decisions and reducing turnover through strategic people insights. Available on Google Play Store and Apple App Store, the app uses AI to match candidates with company culture and team dynamics.

## Challenges

- Creating smooth, engaging animations for mindfulness and mental health exercises
- Implementing offline-first functionality so the app works without a connection
- Building secure, encrypted storage for sensitive personal and psychological assessment data
- Optimizing performance for low-end Android devices common in Latin America

## Solutions

- Used Flutter's animation framework and Lottie for engaging, fluid UX
- Implemented SQLite with a sync queue for reliable offline-first data persistence
- Applied end-to-end encryption for all sensitive user data at rest and in transit
- Used lazy loading and image caching to maintain 60fps on mid-range devices

## Results

- 4.8-star rating on Google Play Store
- 10,000+ downloads in the first month after launch
- 85% user retention rate at 30 days
- Featured as a top productivity app in Ecuador's Play Store
